Sweet & Spicy Jalapeño Poppers

This recipe is slightly modified from this version from Taste of Home. It's always a big hit at parties so I always make a double batch! Don't be fooled by the jalapeños--this recipe isn't spicy at all thanks to the cheese blend, but you can mix the seeds in with the filling if you'd like a stronger kick. These are also great with a pinch of cayenne pepper in the seasoning mix.

Ingredients

  • 12 Jalapeño Peppers
  • 1-8oz Package of Cream Cheese
  • 4 tbsp Sharp Cheddar (shredded)
  • 1/4 cup Packed Brown Sugar
  • 1 tbsp Chipotle Chili Powder
